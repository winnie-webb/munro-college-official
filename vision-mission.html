The Vision

To provide an enviable and enriching learning environment that facilitates the successful development of intelligent and disciplined students who can attain highest levels of achievement in their educational and social pursuits in order to become respectable and contributing memebers of society through the commitment of a well-trained staff and supportive Board and parents.

The Mission

To develop young men of sterling character who display excellence in academic, social, emotional, physical, spiritual, moral and co-curricular activities in a safe, caring well-disciplined environment that will enable them to graduate with requisite knowledge, skills and attitudes to succeed in a global economy.
